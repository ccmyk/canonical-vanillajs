Villal\u00f3n Studio Website<\/h2><\/div>

How do we inhabit spaces? How does a person interact when he or she is part of it? These were the questions we were looking for answers to.\u2028Through a website with a strong presence of white spaces and images, we give rise to a dialogue and a dynamism that is felt throughout the experience. <\/p>\n

A navigation that tries to take us to a calmer pace and reflection of space, where a dialogue between the studio and the user takes place. A direct and contemporary aesthetic, without flourish, with which to leave a mark.\u2028<\/p>\n
